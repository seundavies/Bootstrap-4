ABOUT DATOBEX

Datobex Technology Limited is an incorporated company registered in Nigeria with operational office in Lagos, Nigeria.


We pride ourselves as a premium Telecommunication and Information Technology Provider with aim to offer lasting solutions to major related matters by offering cutting edge services to our clients.


We offer our client the complete installation package, include consultancy, installation, support and supply some IT/Telecom accessories. Our wide ranging expertise and emerging technologies. The undertake all size of network installation (server room, wireless LAN, structural cabling) to provide a total service project anywhere in Nigeria. Network installation include complete Local Area Network utilizing copper, fiber and wireless Technologies as well as simple additional to existing live network.

Contract can be carried out with very short lead times and with the flexibility to work out of normal the hours if required.
